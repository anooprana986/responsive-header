Dhriiti is an idea of building an entrepreneurial & proactive society and nation state. We believe entrepreneurs are the most diligent change makers trying to make a better world and hence Dhriiti invests in inspiring & incubating entrepreneurs who build transformative enterprises creating change in the society and nation state.

Info Graphic of Inspire, Incubate and Transform

Dhriiti creates Financially, Socially and Ecologically (FSE) impactful entrepreneurs and enterprises to generate sustainable livelihoods.

We work with marginalized communities across geographies.
